## The Mystery

Pay attention to what's wrong:
- Newspaper has no date
- Sea is completely still (frozen in time)
- Elena's face blurs when you look directly at it
- Logbooks span 1897 to 2089
- "Day 1" written hundreds of times
- White void where the world should be

**The truth reveals itself if you notice the details.**
