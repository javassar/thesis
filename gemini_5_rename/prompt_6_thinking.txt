  Playtest Narrative:


  I launch the game, greeted by a black screen and top-left instructions: "Move
  with Arrow Keys. Jump with Up Arrow. Click objects to rename them (e.g.,
  Wall.ghost, Hazard.ice, User.gravity_0)." I see my "User.png" character and a
  platform. Moving and jumping works.


  I encounter a red "Hazard.fire." I try jumping over it, collide, and nothing
  happens. Confused, why no immediate feedback? I walk into it again. "Burned!
  Game Over!" flashes, and the game restarts. Frustrating, I died without
  immediate understanding.


  Restarting, I rename "Hazard.fire" to "Hazard.ice." It turns light blue and
  feels slippery to traverse. Continuing right, I see "Wall.static," "Wall.zip,"
  and "Exit.shortcut." I rename "Wall.static" to "Wall.ghost," and it disappears,
  allowing passage. I then rename "Wall.zip" to "Wall.door," which animates by
  shrinking and vanishing. I proceed to "Exit.shortcut," walking into it triggers
  a camera zoom-out, revealing a black screen with "User.png." I assume I've won.


  I restart to experiment with self-renaming. I rename "User.png" to
  "User.gravity_0." I can now float over obstacles. Reaching "Exit.shortcut"
  triggers the win animation again.

  The core mechanics are engaging, but two issues arose:

  Playability Issues:


   1. Hazard.fire feedback:
       * What went wrong: Colliding with "Hazard.fire" lacked immediate danger
         cues, leading to a confusing, delayed "Game Over."
       * Root cause: hitHazard immediately pauses physics and shows "Game Over"
         without prior visual/auditory warnings.
       * Fix: When the player touches "Hazard.fire," the player will flash red,
         and after a short delay, "Game Over" will trigger.


   2. Win condition clarity/replayability:
       * What went wrong: The win animation ended with a static desktop and no
         explicit "You Win!" message or option to restart.
       * Root cause: winGame lacked a clear victory message and restart
         mechanism.
       * Fix: Add a "You Win!" message and a clear option to restart the game.
